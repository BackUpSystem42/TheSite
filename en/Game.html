No Sequel Work Model.

In other words, each game is a standalone experience. Each one is not related nor connect to each other except for the creator.

So why bother, if it is not a series or a one off. I am a one trick pony.

Well, as of May 22, 2026, I have 24 game ideas. I have a few sketches, sample description, and a few unfinished projects. I am not just an idea guy. I was working on a few ideas/stories and some major edits have to be made.

  • This character’s motive or personality changes too much. They basically become a new character.
  • This character has too much ability and is overpowered.
  • The general theme of the overall story was not goal, but I like the idea
  • A specific arc or event is more impactful an original expected, but it was not the original goal
  • I have more to tell and about this location than originally expected

So instead of forcing one story to have all these elements that I know will clash. I have made serval different stories so I can these individual concepts breath and grow.

There are basically three main architects for the stories

A robot felled with sadness

A person wangling a carnival of goofs

A person with a mask that knows a little too much

I am not giving a name since I am not sure which project will be worked on next, is it doable yet, or I do not have a good name for it. But here are a few teaser images.
